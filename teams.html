Indian Society of Technical Education.

Technical Prophet is a brainchild of potterhead and geek, Dhiman Chatterjee. Started in May 2019, the blog promotes critical thinking ability, linguistic development of members of ISTE and all things technical.

The Indian Society for Technical Education is a national, professional, non-profit Society. It has active membership of more than 97286 technical teachers, 5,66,466 student members, more than 2345 institutional members (including IITs, IISc.., NITs and other leading technical institutions), 1166 faculty chapters and 1280 students’ chapters throughout the country. The major objective of the ISTE is to assist and contribute in the production and development of top quality professional engineers and technicians needed by the industries and other organisations. Being the only national organisation of educators in the field of Engineering and Technology, ISTE effectively contributes in various missions of the Union Government. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, CTE/Department of Science and Technology/MIT/State Govts. are well associated with the ISTE for programmes relating to technical education.

ISTE Manipal is a student chapter under Indian Society For Technical Education. This multidomain club organises workshops and events for tech aficionados.ISTE organizes various events throughout an academic year. The workshops and events organized by the club prove very usefull for the students of MIT and they get to learn a lot. A common feedback received by the club is that they organize very informative workshops and interesting events.

ISTE organizes various workshops such as Intro to GIT, Python: basic to advance, OpenCV and much more. Apart from workshops ISTE has its own Tech Week.This is club’s flagship event of even semester. The TechWeek brings in all the different domains of engineering into a series of events. TechWeek has many events like “Watt Up”, “ExQUIZite”, “Clueless”, “Unlockathon” and “Clash of Codes”. The TechWeek had a participation of over 1200 people including both Online and Offline event.

ISTE mission is to promote technical growth among students of India.ISTE also has a lot to offer even if you're not in the campus.In summer and winter breaks ISTE organizes summer and winter school which is a crash course of latest technologies taught by none other than your own seniors. If all that was not enough then guess what ISTE is the only club to organize on-demand workshops.
